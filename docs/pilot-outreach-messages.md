## 4. In-person pitch (30–60 seconds)

Use this at networking events, trade meetups, or when you run into a business owner locally.

```
"I help small service businesses stop losing website leads.

SiteAgentAI is like an AI employee on your website — it talks to visitors, collects their contact info and what they need, and gives you a summary with a recommended next step. So instead of a vague form submission, you open your dashboard and know exactly who to call.

I've got a live demo on my phone if you want to see it — takes about two minutes. We're doing early pilots at forty-nine a month for the first group of owners."
```

**If they say yes to the demo:** walk through widget → submit test lead → show `/admin`.

**If they say they're busy:** "No problem — can I text you the link? Takes two minutes on your own time."

## Quick tips

- **Personalize one line** — mention their business name, city, or service type
- **Lead with the demo link** — owners understand faster by seeing it
- **Ask for honest feedback** — "not for us" is still useful validation
- **Don't oversell** — you're testing fit, not closing enterprise deals
- **One clear ask per message** — view demo, reply with thoughts, or book a call
